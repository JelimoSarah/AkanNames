AKAN NAMES - A GHANAIN CULTURE

This is a page that enables one to know their Akan name, a Ghanain culture.

In Ghana, children are named according to the day they were born.

Males are given certain and women likewise. For example :

  1. Sunday: Female - Akosua Male - Kwasi
  2. Monday: Female - Adwoa Male - Kwadwo

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.

Information Required:

What is your gender?Pick one.

What is your birthdate?Write in the format of DDMMCCYY where CC stands for century and YY stands for last part of year.e.g in 09/11/1989(9th November 1989) 09 is date,11 is month, 19 is century and 89 is year.
